About this plan

It is a provision introduced under the Income Tax Act in India, allowing taxpayers to correct mistakes or omissions in previously filed ITR. The ITR-U helps rectify errors such as missing income, wrong claims or incorrect details.

Who Should File Belated ITR-U?

  • Missed filing return within due date.
  • Need to add missed income from previous filing.
  • Need to correct incorrect details in filed return.
  • Received notice to regularize prior filing mismatch.

Late Filing Impact

  • Additional tax and interest may apply.
  • Delay can increase compliance follow-up risk.
  • Future loan/profile verification can be affected.
  • Timely correction helps reduce notice stress.

Documents Required

Identity & Profile

PAN, Aadhaar, mobile and email linked to income tax portal.

Income & Bank

Form 16/salary slips, bank statements, and other income proofs.

Deductions & Investments

Proofs for 80C/80D, loan statements, and deduction documents.

Previous Return Data

Earlier ITR acknowledgement and any notice/communication copies.
